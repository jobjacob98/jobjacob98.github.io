About the Theme

Refashioning the Elementary

The concept of origin is strong in the "original," and the existing knowledge comprises of previous knowledge in different combinations and permutations, nuancing itself to the contemporary. The process of rediscovering, reinventing goes on...

These days with a plethora of new ideas sprouting, with the social media at finger's touch, sometimes we lose focus on pivotal points that made daring departures in thought. Here is an attempt to retrace them... the elementary has to be refashioned to the needs and challenges of the day. Here is a platform offered for a thought revolution that can problematize the straight-jacketed stereotypes and bring in a whiff of fresh air to rethink and reinvent.

The primary aim of the theme, refashioning the elementary, is to provoke you to make use of some time to re-think, deconstruct, redefine, give colors and add luster to orthodox and conventional ideas along with those ideas neglected due to ignorance in order to transform them into efficacious and futuristic breakthroughs.
